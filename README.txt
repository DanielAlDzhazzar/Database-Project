User Guide:

On first entrance to the website, register a new user by entering all required data in the following fields:
Name must be 3–50 characters long and contain only letters and spaces.
Email must be a valid format (something@something.com), 5–50 characters long and unique.
Phone number must contain 10–13 digits only and be unique.
Passport must follow the format of 2 uppercase letters followed by 6 digits (AB123456) and be unique.
Password must be 8–25 characters long and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.

ForAfter registration to login, click on “Have an account already? Enter” and provide your email and password.

After authorization, the following options become available: Take a Loan, Profile, Logout.

Logout ends your current session.

Profile contains your personal data, loans, payment section, and statistics:
Personal section displays your registration data.
Loans section shows all your loans, both active and completed.
Payment section allows you to select a loan and make a payment. Before making a payment, you must add a debit card using the button provided. After adding card details successfully, you can enter a payment amount (greater than 0). The system will also display the percentage of the loan already paid.
Statistics section provides aggregated information about your loans.

Take a Loan allows you to apply for a loan with the following conditions:
Minimum loan amount is 1000 euros.
Loans below 10000 euros have a 5% interest rate and a maximum term of 6 months.
Loans between 10000 and 50000 euros have a 3% interest rate with available terms of 6 or 12 months.
Loans above 50000 euros have a 2% interest rate with available terms of 6, 12, 18, or 24 months.
Info and Contact sections contain information about the company and contact details.
